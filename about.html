Hello, I'm Angela!

I went to Brandeis University and triple-majored (yes, I know) in Computer Science, Economics, and East Asian Studies. I was convinced I would end up as a software engineer, but did a lot of art/design on the side, having loved all things creative since childhood. When I discovered UX/UI design during my junior year, I knew immediately that was what I wanted to do. The reason why I studied CS in the first place was because of technology's ability to solve problems and help people; design is what provides the genuine empathy and creativity that technology needs to do so. Since then, I've been on a self-motivated journey where I've expanded my design skills and thinking through various projects and work experience.

Currently, I'm working as a UX/UI Designer for NESN, the New England Sports Network. I am helping to redesign and expand its ever-growing digital presence which impacts millions of people across the country. I'm always looking to connect with others, so feel free to reach out for collaborations or just chat! :)
