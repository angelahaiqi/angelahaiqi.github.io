Hello, I'm Angela!

I'm a recent graduate of Brandeis University, with a degree in Computer Science, Economics, and East Asian Studies (I know. I don't sleep). Growing up near New York City, I was constantly inspired by museums, street art, architecture, and every other quirk it had to offer. I was inspired to try new things and see things from different perspectives. That's why I've always loved creating (and majored in three different fields). Art and design allow me to express myself and have shaped the way I view the world. They are everywhere, from the cars we drive to the buildings we work and live in.

I am a part of a generation where we saw technology evolve so rapidly. I've been able to understand how it influences people's decisions and can be leveraged to solve problems. My fascination with this propels me to pursue a career in Product and UX/UI Design, the intersection of my passions: creative pursuits and technology.

I'm currently looking for a full-time job! My ability to put myself in the user's shoes allows me to tackle complex problems and deliver compelling solutions. I have a natural curiosity and drive to learn, having taught myself design tools and HTML/CSS. I'm always looking to connect and collaborate with others, so feel free to reach out to discuss opportunities or just chat! :)
