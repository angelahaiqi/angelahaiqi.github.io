Hello, I'm Angela!

I'm a senior at Brandeis University, triple-majoring in Computer Science, Economics, and East Asian Studies (I know. I don't sleep). Growing up near New York City, I was constantly inspired by museums, street art, architecture, and every quirk it had to offer. I was inspired to try new things and see things from different perspectives. That's why I've always loved creating. Art and design allow me to express myself and have shaped the way I view the world. They are everywhere, from the cars we drive to the buildings we work and live in.

I am a part of a generation where we saw technology evolve so rapidly. I've been able to understand the importance in thinking about how people interact with it. My drive to create meaningful experiences for digital products propels me to pursue a career in UX/UI design, the intersection of my passions: creative pursuits and technology.

I'm looking for opportunities after I graduate in May! I am self-taught in Adobe, prototyping tools, and HTML/CSS. With experience in desiging for various organizations, I have the drive and skills to tackle complex problems and deliver compelling solutions. I'm always eager to learn more and love collaborating with others!
