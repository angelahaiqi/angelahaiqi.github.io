New England Sports Network

Currently, I am working as a UX/UI Designer at NESN, one of the top regional sports networks in America.

Role: UX/UI Designer

Date: October 1st 2020 - Present

Tools: Sketch, Photoshop, InVision

Highlights

In October 2020, I was onboarded as an Associate UX/UI Designer for NESN's Digital Team. I collaborate (mostly) remotely with the Senior Designer, Product Manager, web developers, and other stakeholders to develop new ideas for NESN's website, connected app, and mobile app. My responsibilities include:

1. Leveraging feedback and insights, I iteratively create low and high-fidelity wireframes/mockups. My designs are either visual and/or usability improvements of existing pages and features, or visions for new ones. Some of my designs have already been implemented (you may have seen them!) or will be implemented in the future. One particularly successful design has helped increase engagement by up to 200%.

2. I conduct research to gather and evaluate user needs. This allows for the Digital Team to approach problems strategically.

3. I respond to submitted UI/UX requests from stakeholders and contribute to other smaller-scale projects as assigned. Some tasks help to streamline internal processes, others support the work of other teams.
